Application for Bio-techno workshop++exhibition

Deadline to apply: the 6th of January 2025

Schedule:
1-2nd of February from 10:30 till 17:30
Bio-techno workshop - you will learn about electronics, circuits, programming and how it can intersect with art/science. You will develop small projects using materials provided. This is meant to be a hackathon (rapid and collaborative prototyping) and we will be looking at making fun projects, rather than finished pieces.


3rd of February at 19:00
Pop up exhibition - we will present what we made together in Filet gallery for one night.


What will you get:
Every participant will get a robotics kit to take home, budget for their project. + communal lunches, tea, coffee, snacks on both days. Accessibility needs will be met.
++ It is going to be a fun experience, you can meet people, learn something new and present your project.


Equipment:
Participants are asked to bring their own computers, but a limited number of computers are available if needed.


Everyone is welcome to apply. People who especially feel excluded from such spaces, opportunities and topic are encouraged to apply!


Where:
FILET gallery, London
103 Murray Grove
